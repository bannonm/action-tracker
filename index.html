App Instructions
What is Altius?

Altius is a voice-first project log for construction and development professionals. Use it to capture action items, meetings, and site events in real time — from the field, in your truck, or in a meeting room. Every item is timestamped, linked to a project, and synced across all your devices instantly.

Logging an Item

Tap the microphone button at the bottom of the screen. Speak naturally — Claude AI parses your speech into structured fields. You'll see a confirmation card before anything is saved. Tap Save or say "yes" to confirm.

You can also type directly in the text box if voice is not practical.

Item Types

Task — something that needs to be done. Claude assigns this when you describe a follow-up or deliverable.

Meeting — a future or recurring meeting. Include who is attending and when.

Event — something that has already happened. Use this for site visits, incidents, inspections, or conversations you want on record.

Voice Commands Reference
LOG AN ITEM

"Meeting with Brent and Sarah from Legendary next Friday to review the foundation RFI. Due end of month."

LOG WITH TIME RANGE

"Create an event from 7am to 1pm today — site inspection at Bluewater Marina with ABC Electric."

ADD A PROJECT

"Add a new project called Riverside Commons."

TEACH AN ALIAS

"Remember that Legendary means Bluewater Marina."

"Remember that Brent means Brent Creary from ArchVeture."

EDIT AN ITEM

"Change the due date on the ABC Electric foundation meeting to May 15th."

DELETE AN ITEM

"Delete the task about the RFI for Bluewater Marina."

CREATE MEETING INVITE

"Schedule a meeting with Brent next Tuesday at 2pm and send a calendar invite."

Aliases

Aliases let you use shorthand names that Altius automatically resolves. Say "Legendary" and Altius logs it as "Bluewater Marina." Add aliases in Settings or by voice. Include email addresses to enable automatic population of calendar invites.

Calendar

Switch between Month, Week, and Day views. Tap a day in Month view then switch to Day view to jump directly to that date. Events with start and end times appear as time blocks in Week and Day view.

Share & Export

Export to Excel — downloads all items as a spreadsheet including all people, companies, times, and duration. Use for billing analysis and reporting.

Meeting Invite — select any item to generate an ICS calendar file. iOS opens it directly in your Calendar app, pre-populated with attendees whose email addresses are stored in your alias list.

App Instructions

What is Altius?

Altius is a voice-driven project action item tracker built for construction and real estate development professionals. Log meeting notes, tasks, and events hands-free while on site, in the car, or between meetings. Every item is time-stamped, linked to a project, and synced across all your devices instantly.

Getting Started

1. Add your projects — Go to Settings → Projects and add each project by name. Altius will automatically match your speech to the correct project.

2. Add aliases — Go to Settings → Aliases to teach Altius shorthand. Add each person's full name, company, and email so you can say "Brent" and it logs "Brent Creary from ArchVeture".

3. Start logging — Tap the microphone button and speak naturally. Altius handles the rest.

Item Types

Task — Something that needs to be done. Altius assigns a responsible party and due date.

Meeting — A scheduled future meeting. Attendees and time are logged. Generate a calendar invite from the Share screen.

Event — Something that has already happened. Start and end time are recorded for billing purposes.

Voice Commands

Tap the mic and speak naturally. Examples:

Log an item

"Meeting with Brent and Sarah from Legendary next Friday to review the foundation specs for Bluewater Marina. Sarah is responsible."

Log with time

"Site inspection event from 7am to 1pm today at Riverside Commons. Billed to ABC Electric."

Add a project

"Add a new project called Riverside Commons"

Create an alias

"Remember that Legendary means Bluewater Marina" or "Remember that Brent means Brent Creary from ArchVeture"

Edit an item

"Change the due date on the ABC Electric foundation task to May 15th"

Delete an item

"Delete the overdue task for John Smith"

Create a meeting invite

"Meeting with Brent next Tuesday at 10am and send a meeting invite"

Tips

• Silent mode — tap the speaker icon in the header to mute voice responses during meetings.

• Typing — tap the mic button and type instead of speaking when voice is not practical.

• Calendar invites — go to Share → Meeting Invite, or swipe left on any item and tap Share.

• Excel export — Share → Export to Excel. Includes start/end times and duration for billing.

• Editing items — tap any item tile to open the full edit card.

• Swipe left on any item to reveal Edit, Share, and Delete.
